Cash or check payments can be paid to Our Treasurer or Club President by appt at the field or at the meetings.

The preferred method to renew or join the Hawks is to pay your club dues via PayPal at this address: rc.hawks.treasurer@gmail.com using "Send money to friends and family" to avoid the Hawks paying a service charge. If your PayPal account is not your legal name please add your legal name in the "Add a note" field of your PayPal payment so we can properly record the payment.
